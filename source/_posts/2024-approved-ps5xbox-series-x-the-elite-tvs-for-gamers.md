* **Refresh Rate:** 120Hz
* **Adaptive Sync:** G-Sync, FreeSync Premium

LG's C1 gaming console is designed to take on the latest generation of gaming consoles. For smooth and sharp gaming, it has a 4K display with a 120Hz refresh rate.
